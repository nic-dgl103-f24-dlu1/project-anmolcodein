About Woodland Gardens

Kitty Coleman Woodland Gardens, founded by Bryan Zimmerman, began as a vision to share the natural beauty of his land with others. Zimmerman, who operated a Christmas tree farm and Santa's Barn in the Comox Valley, saw the potential for something more when he started clearing the underbrush on his 24-acre forested property. Over the years, he transformed the land into a woodland sanctuary filled with a network of cedar bark paths, small ponds, and over 3,000 rhododendrons.
